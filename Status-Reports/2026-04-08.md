### 1. CIRCULAR REFERENCE ON 51.1142
- **Root cause:** Vista ContractStatus=3 (HardClosed) passed raw to VBA which only handles 1/2. The 2→3 transition fired subtotal logic incorrectly, creating SUM ranges that included themselves.
- **Fix:** ContractStatus now mapped to 1 (Open) or 2 (Closed) in SQL. Michael's LCGWIPCreateBatch used same 1/2 mapping.
- **Status:** FIXED — no circular references.

### 2. CLOSED JOBS IN ROWS 37-44 SHOWING WHEN THEY SHOULDN'T
- **Root cause:** Our EXISTS clauses caught $0 ghost transactions (SL records with ActualCost=0) pulling in long-closed jobs.
- **Fix:** HardClosed jobs now only included if MonthClosed falls within the WIP year (Jan 1 – Dec 1). Matches Michael's filter. Also added zero-activity/zero-contract exclusion.
- **Status:** FIXED — 7 HardClosed Dec 2024 jobs excluded. 51.1139 (HardClosed Dec 2025) correctly retained.

### 3. SUBTOTALS AND TOTALS SHOWING $0
- **Root cause:** Override columns (I=Revenue, M=Cost) were left at $0 when no explicit override existed. All downstream formulas (% Complete, Earned Revenue, Projected Profit) depend on I/M.
- **Fix:** When no override, default I to Vista projected revenue and M to MAX(Vista projected cost, actual cost). Matches Michael's LCGWIPCreateBatch defaulting logic.
- **Status:** FIXED — all formulas now evaluate correctly.

### 4. JOB 51.1158 COLUMN W = $0, SHOULD BE $56,961
- **Root cause:** Same as #3 (override columns empty), plus template circular formula in Column Z (W→Z→Y→W chain). VBA now always writes a value to Column Z, breaking the chain.
- **Fix:** Override defaulting (#3) + explicit Column Z write.
- **Status:** FIXED — Column W shows $56,960.69 ($56,961).

### 5. JOB 51.1139 MISSING FROM WIP
- **Nicole's feedback:** Job is closed and had a $481 profit change in 2025 in order to make the job 100% complete and closed. If a job was open on the prior year's (12/31/24) WIP, it has to appear on the following year's WIP regardless of cost/billing status.
- **Root cause:** Job was included by the HardClosed-in-WIP-year filter (MonthClosed = Dec 2025) but was sorted into the Open section instead of Closed. Nicole confirmed on follow-up call: it belongs in Closed.
- **Fix:** ContractStatus mapping changed to show Vista-closed jobs in the Closed section. The $481 CY revenue change is visible in Column AL.
- **Status:** FIXED — 51.1139 in Closed section with correct override values and $4,907.56 bonus profit.
